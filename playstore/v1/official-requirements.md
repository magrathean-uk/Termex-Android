# Official Play Requirements

Main sources:

- [Add preview assets to showcase your app](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9866151?hl=en)
- [Create and set up your app](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9859152?hl=en)
- [Google Play icon design specifications](https://developer.android.com/google-play/resources/icon-design-specifications)
- [Build and test your Android App Bundle](https://developer.android.com/guide/app-bundle/test)

Key requirements used for this pack:

- App name: max 30 characters.
- Short description: max 80 characters.
- Full description: max 4000 characters.
- Support email: required.
- Website: strongly recommended.
- Privacy policy URL: required for this app class.
- Play icon:
  - 512 x 512
  - 32-bit PNG
  - alpha allowed
  - max 1024 KB
- Feature graphic:
  - 1024 x 500
  - JPEG or 24-bit PNG
  - no alpha
- Screenshots:
  - minimum 2 screenshots across device types to publish
  - recommended 4 phone screenshots for app promotion
  - phone shots should be at least 1080 x 1920 for 9:16 portrait
  - must show real in-app experience
- AAB:
  - use signed release bundle
  - Play internal testing gives the most accurate delivery proof

Metadata rules kept here:

- No ranking claims.
- No price or promo language in title, icon, screenshots, or graphic.
- No call-to-action text like “download now”.
- Keep screenshots and text aligned to shipped features only.
